About the Group

Who We Are

MaxNova is a UK-based group founded by Max Dorman, built around the belief that businesses deserve better technology and better design — and that the two should work together seamlessly.

We operate two specialist companies under the MaxNova umbrella: MaxNova Tech, which provides managed IT services and technology solutions for businesses across the UK, and MaxNova Creative, a web design and graphic art studio delivering custom-built websites and brand identities.

Everything we do is built from scratch, tailored to the client, and designed to actually perform — not just look good on a slide deck.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that run through everything we build, across both companies.

01

No Shortcuts.

We don't cut corners — in code, in design, or in support. Everything is built properly, the first time.

02

Built to Perform.

Fast, reliable, and optimised. Whether it's a network or a website, it needs to work in the real world — not just look good in a demo.

03

Client First.

We work with you, not just for you. Clear communication, honest advice, and results you can actually measure.

04

Genuinely Bespoke.

No templates, no one-size-fits-all solutions. Every client is different — and what we build for you reflects that.

05

Always Improving.

Technology and design move fast. We keep up so our clients don't have to — always learning, always updating.

06

Honest Pricing.

No hidden fees, no surprise invoices. We scope everything upfront and stick to it.
